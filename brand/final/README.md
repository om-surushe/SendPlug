# SendPlug brand

## Core idea

Two product modules snap together around a sending envelope:

- **Modules:** plug-and-play integration
- **Envelope and forward point:** sending email
- **Joined center:** SendPlug sits between the founder's application and email delivery

## Positioning

**Category:** Plug-and-play email infrastructure for new-age founders.

**Primary line:** Plug email into your product.

**Product rhythm:** Plug in. Send. See.

The three verbs map to the product:

1. **Plug in** — npm package, API token, and Google connection
2. **Send** — transactional and permission-based product email
3. **See** — dashboard, delivery history, analytics, quotas, and suppression

## Files

- `sendplug-mark-dark.svg` — standalone mark on dark surfaces
- `sendplug-mark-light.svg` — standalone mark on light surfaces

- `sendplug-lockup-light.svg` — wordmark for light surfaces

- `sendplug-favicon.svg` — simplified browser icon
- `png/` — raster exports
- `sendplug-logo-preview.html` — browser preview and size checks

## Colors

- Near black: `#090909`
- Electric yellow: `#FAFF69`
- Soft white: `#F7F7F7`
- Muted gray: `#8D8D8D`

## Rules

- Use the dark lockup on near-black surfaces.
- Use the light lockup on white or electric-yellow surfaces.
- Keep clear space around the mark equal to one connector notch.
- Do not add gradients, shadows, outlines, or extra colors.
- Below 32 px, use `sendplug-favicon.svg` rather than the detailed mark.
